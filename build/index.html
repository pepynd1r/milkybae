The Story

Origins

In the center of the Milky Way, a cataclysmic event is happening at this very moment. A Black Hole at the center of our galaxy is devoured from within. The powerful Milky Baes are escaping its gravity field leaving nothing but destruction and terror behind. Their appearance is godlike, however, their intentions are world domination and nothing is capable of getting in their way. Best believe, there is no stopping them, and your only chance to survive is if you own a Milky Bae NFT, which might just save your life.

The Makin of

Origin story aside, this NFT collection compares to no other. All of our attributes and main elements were drawn by hand on a rice paper canvas, colored with the highest quality paint. Don't believe us? Check out the gallery to your left!

After the artworks were completed, they were digitalized and assembled together. We used photo editing software as little as possible because we wanted to keep the authentic look.
